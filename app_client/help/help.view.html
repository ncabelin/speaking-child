WELCOME TO THE SPEAKING CHILD HELP SECTION

Words / Phrases

  1. Make sure you Register for a new account first. Then go to Login.
  2. After logging in, you'll be redirected to the 'Words' dashboard. This is where you can add words. Click and a form will appear.
  3. Enter 1 word (or 2 or more words to enter a phrase). 'Sound' is optional, if you don't fill it up then word and sound will be the same value (entering a sound can give you a way to figure out which words do not sound right for later correction/practice).
  4. Clicking 'Guide' will show you some recommendations of words to practice with your child
  5. 'Guide' words will show up in a green color if you've entered it into the database.

Guide

  1. Clicking the 'Guide' tab will show you some recommendations of words to practice with your child
  2. 'Guide' words will show up in a green color if you've already entered it into the database.
  3. 'Guide' words that show up in white are the ones you haven't entered into the database.
  4. Click a word to add it into your database

Chart

  1. Clicking 'Chart' on the menu will show you a bar chart of your child's progress.
  2. On the bottom, a table chart of monthly speech progress (number of words) will be shown.
  3. More interactive features will be developed in the future, so stay tuned...
